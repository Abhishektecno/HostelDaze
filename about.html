Objective

The Hostler Students Permission Letter web application is designed to help students living in hostels or dormitories to obtain permission letters from their hostel authorities for various reasons such as leave of absence, attending an event, or visiting family members. The application is intended to simplify the permission letter process and reduce the time and effort required to obtain permission. The application would allow students to create an account and log in to the system. Students would then be able to fill out a form with details of the permission they require, including the reason for the permission, the duration of the permission, and any supporting documents or information required. The application would also provide an option for students to upload any necessary documents or information.
